Learning

At Kids Den School, we believe that learning should be exciting, interactive, and meaningful. As the first school in Mizoram to introduce advanced smart boards in every classroom, we’ve created a tech-enabled environment where lessons come alive through visual and audio support, transforming the way children engage with education.

Backed by a dedicated team of educators, we cultivate a learning environment where play, creativity, and critical thinking are central to every child’s growth. We are passionate about providing a rich, stimulating curriculum that supports both prime and specific areas of development, fostering skills that go beyond the classroom. At Kids Den, we believe every child deserves the chance to live a curious, confident, and creative life—one that prepares them to thrive socially, culturally, academically, and spiritually in an ever-changing world.

Curriculum

At Kids Den School, our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to support each stage of a child’s learning journey.

For preschoolers, we follow our own unique curriculum—carefully developed and refined over 13 years of experience—which continues to evolve to meet the needs of young learners. This early foundation blends play-based learning with age-appropriate academics, nurturing curiosity, confidence, and a love for learning from the very start.

From Kindergarten to Class VIII, we adopt the curriculum prescribed by the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE), ensuring academic excellence while remaining rooted in local relevance.

Mizo, the local language, is a compulsory subject, and moral values, life skills, general knowledge, and information technology are woven into the core of our educational program.

This holistic approach empowers students to grow not only academically, but also socially, culturally, and ethically.

Teaching & Learning

At Kids Den School, the interactive smart board is an integral part of our daily classroom experience. Each lesson is brought to life through dynamic visuals, animations, audio, and hands-on activities that make learning more engaging and effective. Teachers use the smart board to illustrate complex concepts, display educational videos, and conduct interactive quizzes.

This technology-driven approach caters to different learning styles and keeps students actively involved, turning every class into an exciting opportunity to explore, understand, and grow.

We deeply value the importance of a child's mother tongue in shaping their understanding and identity. Recognizing that language plays a crucial role in learning, especially in early years, we have taken the initiative to teach key subjects like Mathematics in the students' mother tongue—primarily Mizo—for better comprehension and connection. This approach not only strengthens their grasp of concepts but also nurtures cultural pride and linguistic confidence.

Library

The Kids Den School library, officially inaugurated on 22nd May 2024, is a growing hub of knowledge and imagination.

With over 1,000 books already in its collection and more on the way, the library offers a wide range of reading materials to inspire curiosity and a love for reading among students. Managed by our dedicated librarian, Miss Lalawmpuii (M.Lib), the space is thoughtfully organized to support both academic learning and leisure reading in a calm, welcoming environment.

Co-curricular Activities

At Kids Den School, we believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals by blending strong academics with a vibrant co-curricular program. Through our dedicated Club activities, students are encouraged to grow beyond the classroom—developing social skills, intellectual abilities, life skills, moral values, and personal character.

A wide range of activities is offered, including outdoor sports like football, basketball, and athletics, as well as indoor games such as badminton, chess, and board games. Creative and intellectual pursuits like debate, creative writing, quiz competitions, and on-the-spot poetry writing help sharpen young minds.

To further support self-expression and creativity, the school publishes a monthly bulletin Campus Crafts and an annual magazine Campus Clusters, providing students with a platform to showcase their literary and artistic talents. In addition, specialized classes in taekwondo, dance, and music allow students to explore their passions and discover new talents.

This holistic approach ensures that every Denian is equipped with the confidence, skills, and values needed for success in all areas of life.
