Community

At Kids Den School, curiosity, creativity, and collaboration are at the heart of everything we do. From vibrant classrooms and lively playgrounds to club activities and creative activities, our campus offers students endless opportunities to learn, grow, and shine—both personally and academically.

Located in the heart of Aizawl, our school is a dynamic hub where young minds discover new talents, build friendships, and cultivate skills for life. Whether through sports, music, dance, debate, or writing, every Denian finds a space to explore passions, build confidence, and dream big.

Student Life

We believe that true education nurtures both the mind and the spirit. Rooted in Christian values, we are committed to guiding students toward lives filled with faith, compassion, integrity, and service.

Our school chaplain plays an active role in offering spiritual support, leading prayers, and helping students connect their daily lives with Christian teachings. Through moral education classes, daily reflections, and opportunities for worship, we encourage every child to grow in kindness, humility, and strength of character, preparing them to live purposeful and faith-centered lives.

Events

Throughout the year, we regularly organize a wide range of events—both academic and co-curricular—held at our campus as well as at various halls and venues across the city. From competitions, and exhibitions to sports , project showcases, and cultural programs, every event offers students the chance to explore new talents and celebrate their achievements.

We also host recreational activities, joyful celebrations of Christian festivals, and memorable farewell programs, creating lasting memories and a vibrant school life that nurtures the spirit of community, creativity, and faith.

Health & Safety

At Kids Den School, the health, safety, and well-being of our students are our highest priorities. A trained nurse is available on campus throughout school hours, supported by a well-equipped sick bay stocked with a wide range of medications and first aid supplies to handle minor injuries or illnesses promptly.

To ensure a secure environment, every classroom, hallway, and corner of the campus is under continuous CCTV surveillance, monitored closely for student safety. Additionally, a security guard is stationed at the campus 24/7, providing an extra layer of protection and peace of mind for our students, staff, and families.

Community & Belonging

At Kids Den School, we welcome and nurture a diverse community of young talents. We believe that building a better world begins with creating a caring, inclusive school environment. With this in mind, we work to remove barriers to learning and growth, celebrate the beautiful range of cultures and backgrounds within our community, and foster mutual respect and understanding. Every child at Kids Den is encouraged to feel valued, supported, and truly at home as they embark on their journey of discovery and development.

Campus Publications

Campus Crafts is a monthly bulletin first published on 28th April 2023 with the aim of nurturing, nourishing, and developing the innate literary and artistic talents of Kids Den students. It was launched to revive the joy of reading and to offer a dedicated platform where students can express their creativity through writing and artwork. Every issue reflects the vibrant imagination and unique voices of our young learners, encouraging them to think differently and creatively.

Complementing this, Campus Clusters is our annual magazine, capturing the spirit of the school year through student contributions, achievements, and memorable events. Together, Campus Crafts and Campus Clusters embody the belief that creativity is a gift within every child—one that grows richer the more it is used, as beautifully expressed in the words of Maya Angelou: “You can’t use up creativity. The more you use, the more you have.”
